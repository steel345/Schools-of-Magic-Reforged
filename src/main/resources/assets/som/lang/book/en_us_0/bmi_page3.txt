  Toads may not appear to be very magical, but do not be deceived these little amphibians have very unique eggs that have different properties depending on their species.
<~><~>  Toads can be found in most swamps or mountainous jungles and can be bred using Spider Eyes, one of the toads will then seek a water source to lay their eggs.
<~><~>  If left in the water the eggs will slowly hatch into tadpoles, however the eggs can also be collected, stored, and used for potions, rituals, or crafting.
