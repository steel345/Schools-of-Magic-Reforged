  The fairies are the small, bright spirits of the grove, each a single colour and each with a gift of its own. Strike one and it scatters into a pinch of fairy dust the shade of its wings, which a careful hand can gather and keep. Like much of fae-kind they cannot abide cold iron, and a blade or tool of iron will wound them twice as deeply as any other.
<~><~>  The Moonlit fairy, palest of them all, sheds a soft light through the dark. The Ember fairy carries a spark of flame. The Bloom fairy coaxes flowers to open, while the Sprout fairy hurries crops toward harvest. The Heart fairy mends the hurts of the kind, and the Sunbeam fairy leaves the undead weak and slow.
