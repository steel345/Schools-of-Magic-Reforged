  The Dust fairy turns a hunting monster away from a crouching friend, and the Mist fairy hides herself while blinding whatever draws near. The Tide fairy gathers floating things from the water and lends a sinking soul a breath. The Arcane fairy draws stray motes of experience toward your hand.
<~><~>  The Breeze fairy softens a long fall, and the Burrow fairy turns up small trinkets from the earth. The Moss fairy snares the feet of the wicked. Beware the Spite fairy, who will lift the goods from a careless pocket, and the Shadow fairy, who blinds and slows all who stray into her gloom.
