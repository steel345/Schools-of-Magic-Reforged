  Apart from our own world lies the Faegrove, a soft green country of endless flowers and gentle light. It is the home of the fae, and it can be reached by those who learn the way. The air there hums with aura, so that any working of auramancy comes twice as easily, its power doubled within the grove.
<~><~>  The road begins at an Acolyte Portal, a ring raised in the old manner of the acolytes. Build it and wake it as their rites require, step through, and the grove will open before you. Tread kindly once you are inside, for you walk as a guest in someone else's garden.
