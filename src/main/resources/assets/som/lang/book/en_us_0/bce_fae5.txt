  Here and there the flowers gather into villages, and these are the homes of the Flower Faes. They are not fairies but something larger and gentler, humanoid blooms that walk upright and tend their gardens with patient care.
<~><~>  Their magic is not their own. They borrow it from the pixies that flit among them, and spend it keeping their village green. A Flower Fae will rarely trouble you and asks one thing only in return, that you leave their plants be. Trample their flowers and even the mildest of them will turn against you.
