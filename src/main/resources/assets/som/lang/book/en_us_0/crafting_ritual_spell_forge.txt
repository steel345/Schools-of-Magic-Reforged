  The Spell Forge is where spells are made. It is conjured by ritual from glass, a steel fence, a steel ingot, and crushed hydrangea, and it will not labour unless it sits atop a lit brazier.<~><~>  Within it are two chambers. The fire below heats the magical components packed into the lower chamber, melting them down into pure magic.<~><~>  That magic rises into the upper chamber, where it collides with the other magic gathered there and wraps itself about the waiting paper. So bound, the paper becomes spell notes, a custom spell, or a scroll.
