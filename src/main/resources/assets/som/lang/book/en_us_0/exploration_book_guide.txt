  Should you wish to range further afield, seek out a copy of the Adventurer's Codex. It gathers what is known of the old ruins and hidden worlds, and of the wonders and dangers that wait within them.
<~><~>  It is bound at a brazier rather than a workbench. Lay a plain book upon the flames together with a spruce sapling, a piece of lapis, a blank map and a sac of ink, and the heat will draw the pages into one.
