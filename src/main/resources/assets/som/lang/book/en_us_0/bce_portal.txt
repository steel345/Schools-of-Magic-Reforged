  The gate to the Faegrove is raised from Acolyte Wood, the pale timber of the acolyte trees. Stand the wood into a square frame four blocks across and four blocks tall, then hollow the middle so that a window two blocks wide and two blocks tall is left open, ringed by wood on every side.
<~><~>  When the frame stands whole, take an Acolyte Wisp in hand and strike the wood with it as you would set a flint to steel. The frame will catch with a soft light, the window will fill with the colours of the grove, and the way will lie open. Step through, and the Faegrove will receive you.
