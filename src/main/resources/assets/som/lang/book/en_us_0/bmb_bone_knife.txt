  A blade of bone and obsidian, keen enough to part a beast from its rarer pieces. A pig's tail, a bird's heart, a bat's wing, a toad's tongue, a fish's tail, a tentacle, a phoenix's feather; such trophies fall on their own scarcely one kill in a hundred.
<~><~>  Cut the creature down with the Bone Knife and, roughly one time in three, it surrenders its prize. It will also coax a still-beating heart from a slain villager about one time in five.
