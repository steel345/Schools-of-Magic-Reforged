  Long before the forests we know, twelve trees grew from the first soil of the world. From their seeds came every tree that followed, the common and the strange alike, and so they are named the Primal Trees.
<~><~>  Their wood still remembers that old beginning, and it is the only wood that will hold an enchantment without breaking. If you mean to build a podium, a pedestal, or any artifice that must carry magic, it must be cut from a Primal Tree. Lesser timber will simply not answer.
