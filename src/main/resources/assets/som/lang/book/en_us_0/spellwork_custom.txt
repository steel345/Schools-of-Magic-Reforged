  A custom spell is forged in the Spell Forge from three things, set off by an activator. You choose a Shape, a Catalyst, and any Modifiers you wish, then right click a sheet of Paper to begin. Use Parchment in place of paper, and the result is a scroll instead.<~><~>  The Shape decides how the spell leaves your hand, and is set by laying its materials in the Forge. Projectile asks Snowball and Bow; Bolt, Arrow and Ice Shard; Touch, Wool and Glass; Self, an Armor Stand; Wall, Bricks and Peridot; Chain, Chain and Jade; Wave, Armor Stand and Aquamarine; Beam, Nethershard and Chain; Rune, Enchanted Book and Palm Leaves; Starfall, Nethershard with Citrine, Spectromancy gem and a Campfire; Plasma, Lapis Lazuli with Fire Charge and Turquoise; and Focus, a Lapis Block.<~><~>  The Catalyst is the spell's effect. Any cauldron catalyst will serve, that is, any brewing ingredient that yields a potion effect, and whatever effect it would brew becomes the spell's working.<~><~>  Modifiers are extra items that bend the spell further. A Clock lengthens its duration; Putridite lends it greater power; a Comparator hastens its cast; Sugar quickens its recharge; and a Target with Rose Extract makes it home upon its mark.
