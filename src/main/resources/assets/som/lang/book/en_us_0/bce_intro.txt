  You hold the Adventurer's Codex, a traveller's record meant for any magician taking their first steps into the wider world. Magic is not learned at a desk alone. It is dug from old ruins, gathered in strange groves, and carried home from places most folk never dare to walk.
<~><~>  These pages gather what is known of such places and the wonders kept within them. Read them at your leisure, then go and see for yourself, for a thing read is never quite the same as a thing found.
