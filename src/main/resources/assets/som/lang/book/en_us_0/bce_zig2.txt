  At the foot of each ziggurat waits a sacrificial altar built of three stones. Give it what it asks and the sealed door will grind open, and not a moment before.
<~><~>  Beyond lies a maze of narrow halls. Some rooms hide a reward behind a false wall, and others hide nothing but a trap set to catch the greedy. Step lightly, watch the floor, and do not trust a corridor that looks too easy. The builders meant for the careless to fail.
