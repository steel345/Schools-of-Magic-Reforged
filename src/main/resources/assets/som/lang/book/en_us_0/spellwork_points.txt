  Not every trinket holds magic worth the spending. Below are the components the Forge will reckon, and what each is worth.<~><~>  A Magic Plant Seed gives one point to its element. A Crushed Plant gives five to its element, or to its school. Gem Dust gives a generous twenty to its element.<~><~>  Crushed Unicorn Horn gives ten to Magician, and eight each to Spell, Potion, and Ritual. A Wand Core gives ten Magician and twenty Spell. Nether Wart gives eight Potion.
