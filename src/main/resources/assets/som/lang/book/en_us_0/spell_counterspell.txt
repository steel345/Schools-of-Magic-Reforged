  This spell hurls a mote of golden abjuration that hungers for hostile magic.<~><~>  Should it strike an incoming spell, the bolt seizes that magic and flings it back along the path it came, returning the harm to whoever cast it.<~><~>  Against a beam or a spell too tightly held to turn, the golden mote instead unravels it entirely, leaving its caster fumbling for a breath before they may weave again.
