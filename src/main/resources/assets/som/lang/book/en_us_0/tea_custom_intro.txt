  Beyond these set recipes, a teapot will gladly brew far more. Crush almost any herb you gather, and it can be steeped into a tea of its very own.
<~><~>  Pour in water, stir in a single modifier, then add up to three crushed herbs and let it brew.
<~><~>  Every herb carries its own nature, so each can lend a unique effect to the cup. Combine them as you please and experiment freely; there is always some new brew waiting to be discovered.
